What does the editorial board do?
What are the duties of the editorial board members?
The editorial board is a primarily student-run board which is tasked with documenting school events throughout the academic session for the purpose of compiling them into the school magazine each year.
The scope of our duties includes photographing and writing reports for school events as well as designing aesthetically appealing magazine pages.
